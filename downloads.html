    • Pod download page

    We are working on making various 'demo' pods available for your PeARS install. To try them out, just click on the name of a pod, and save the associated CSV file. You can then index that file locally by going to your pear on localhost:8080 and choosing the 'index from file' option on your 'Pod management' page. Once you're done, you're ready to search!

    Note that the 'blurred' pods are not available yet. Remember also, when you search, that those are very small indices. You may not find what you're looking for, because it's simply not indexed! If you get frustrated, join us in our indexing campaign!
